Australia Visa – General Document Requirements
  • The following are the requirements for applying for a visa to Australia:
  • A current original passport that is at least six months old and has three blank pages, as well as any previous passports that may be available;
  • A notarized copy of the entire existing passport, including the back page, the biodata page, and the alterations page;
  • The application for a visa to Australia;
  • Provide two up-to-date coloured photographs that meet the official photo requirements.(contact us)
  • A personal cover letter (for self-employed people on business letterhead or for employed people on plain paper);
  • An original, updated bank statement for the previous six months;
  • Form 16 or income tax returns for the previous three years;
  • Corresponding financial records, including property investments, fixed deposits, and other assets (optional);
  • You might have to go through x-ray and medical exams.
  • Please be aware: The government has very strict rules for photographs; make sure your photos follow these guidelines.
Australia Visa – Additional Requirements Based on Applicant Type
  • For Employees: Leave approval letter from employer (signed and sealed) and salary slips for the last 3 months.
  • For Self-Employed Individuals: Business registration proof (e.g., MOA or partnership deed), company bank statements (6 months), and company IT returns (3 years).
  • For Retired Applicants: Proof of retirement, such as pension book or recent pension statements.
  • For Students: Valid student ID card and a bonafide certificate from the institution.
  • For Minors: Birth certificate, notarized NOC from both/non-accompanying parent(s) on ₹100 stamp paper, and parent ID proof (Passport/PAN card).
  • When Visiting Friends/Relatives: Invitation letter, copy of inviter’s passport or residency permit, and utility bill as proof of residence.
  • If Sponsored: Sponsorship letter, sponsor's valid ID (passport/PAN/residency permit), bank statement (6 months), and IT returns (3 years).
  • For Business Visa: Invitation letter from host company detailing visit purpose and a covering letter from the Indian company on official letterhead.
